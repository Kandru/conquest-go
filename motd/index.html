battlefield style conquest mod

Experience a battlefield like conquest match in CSGO and help your team to win against the enemies. Capture flags and try to hold them. The strongest team will win!

advanced class and ranking system

Like battlefield our mod got a new ranking and class system. Choose between four classes and change them every time. If you play hard you'll rank up and get new weapons which help you to conquer flags. You have to rank up for all four classes separately. You can make rank progress on CT or T side. Only difference between the sides are the available weapons.

four different classes

recon

With weapons like SGG08, G3SG1 and AWP you can simply kill enemies from a far distance and backup your team!

assault

With weapons like Famas, M4A1-S, M4A4 and AUG as well as HE grenades and flashbangs you can push through the enemy lines!

engineer

With weapons like MP9, MP7, PP-Bizon and P90 as well as incendiary and smoke grenades you can support your team as well!

support

With weapons like MP9, Nova, XM1014 and at least two flashbangs you can definately support your team!

how to control conquest:go

some commands are executed after respawn only

change class

Write !c in chat.

change loadout

Write !w in chat. You can change your loadout for T and CT separately.

change skin

As V.I.P. you can change your skin with !s easily.

special weapons

because we want to extend conquest:go as much as possible

medkit

As assault you'll get a bomb at spawn. If you drop this bomb you'll automatically spawn an medkit. A team mate will get 10 healht per second back.

ammobox

As support you'll get a bomb at spawn. If you drop this bomb you'll automatically spawn an ammobox. A team mate will gain 10 ammonition per second.

tugs

As recon you'll get a bomb at spawn. If you drop this bomb you'll automatically spawn an tugs. A tugs will show enemies on the map for at least 30 seconds.


did you know?

You can destroy these boxes as they only have 100 health. They will also disappear after death of the owner. Each box got an limit on health and ammonition that can be given to a player. The owner will get money everytime a team mate uses that box.
